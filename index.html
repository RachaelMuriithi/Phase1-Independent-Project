BODY MASS INDEX (BMI)


Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies
to adult men and women. Calculate your BMI to determine your goal.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5 (Gain weight)
  • Normal weight: 18.5 < BMI < 24.9 (Maintain)
  • Overweight: BMI > 24.9 (Loss weight)

The Calorie Calculator can be used to estimate
